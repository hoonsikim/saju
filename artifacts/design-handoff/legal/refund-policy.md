# Refund Policy

The Saju Deep Reading is a digital product, generated and delivered the moment you complete checkout. We honour a **14-day refund window** from the date of purchase under either of the following conditions:

- The PDF was not delivered to your email within 24 hours and could not be retrieved by re-opening your order link.
- A technical issue on our side prevented the reading from generating (e.g., browser error, server error, corrupted PDF).

Interpretation is subjective by nature. We do not refund a reading on the basis of disagreement with its content, tone, or conclusions — this is consistent with industry norms for digital reading products.

To request a refund, reply to your order email with the order ID (#SJ-…). Refunds are processed back to the original card within 5–10 business days.
